FAB LUXE Residences

FAB LUXE Residences is India's first AQI-managed luxury residential development, created for those who value certainty, wellness, and long-term performance over surface indulgence.

Designed as a low-density, future-ready living environment, FAB LUXE integrates clean air systems, thoughtful planning, and governed execution into everyday life. Every decision, visible or invisible, is engineered to protect health, comfort, and enduring value.

Trust & Governance

Luxury, at its highest level, is built on certainty.

FAB LUXE Residences operates under a rare framework of legal oversight and institutional execution, offering buyers a level of confidence that goes beyond design, amenities, or location.

The project is monitored by the Supreme Court of India, ensuring transparency, accountability, and adherence to defined processes throughout development. Execution is entrusted to NBCC (India) Ltd, one of India's most respected government-owned construction and project management enterprises.

This dual layer of judicial monitoring and institutional execution significantly reduces delivery risk, strengthens legal clarity, and reinforces long-term value.
